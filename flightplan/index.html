Proven Results

Strategic initiatives. Calculated targets.

BluIP’s flight plan is based on the CEC call volume data provided in the RFP. Understanding the data, call volume and traffic, we have created the assumptive analysis below which identifies targets, means and optimal benchmarks.

FACTS: Historical volume indicates consistent growth between 2016-2018 with an assumed steady state in 2019. This volume decreases in 2020 at -59% YOY when applying a monthly calculation.

UNKNOWN: Will volume stabilize at the current -59%, continue to trend downward or will there be achieved growth in 2021?

HOW TO PROCEED: BluIP should assume that call volume will stabilize at current levels but marginally increase within 6-9 months, based on forecasted market trends by both STR and other recovered markets.

WHY THIS MATTERS: BluIP has proposed a plan which addresses cost without revenue return {1}, increased Bonvoy adoptions and introduction of an IVA {2} and implement a long-term AIVA strategy {3}.

The data and projections below are variable based on the assumptions made to create a calculation:

  • Average Handle Time (AHT) was the same in all categories

  • Average monthly volume was used due to partial volume in 2020. This was achieved by dividing all calls and minutes by the number of months measured, then multiplying by 12. 2020, 9 months were used.

  • AIVA can be applied to all call types but recommended to phase in and allow for each stakeholder to understand, measure and qualify success. As a sample, AIVA is applied to non-revenue calls as a solution while still fronting the interaction for all other call types.

  • Regardless of the outcome, it should be understood that AIVA is expected to reduce AHT if a caller engages the process.
